I am a Ph.D. student in Computer Science at [Tsinghua University](https://www.tsinghua.edu.cn/en/), affiliated with the [Institute for Advanced Study](https://www.ias.tsinghua.edu.cn/en/), and advised by Prof. [Heung-Yeung Shum](https://baike.baidu.com/item/%E6%B2%88%E5%90%91%E6%B4%8B/10708178). Before joining Tsinghua, I received my B.Eng. in Computer Science from the [Honors School of Harbin Institute of Technology](https://baike.baidu.com/item/%E5%93%88%E5%B0%94%E6%BB%A8%E5%B7%A5%E4%B8%9A%E5%A4%A7%E5%AD%A6%E8%8B%B1%E6%89%8D%E5%AD%A6%E9%99%A2/3895864), [Harbin Institute of Technology](https://www.hit.edu.cn/) in 2021.

I have been a research intern at [Microsoft Research Asia](https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/research/) since 2020, where I work with Dr. [Yang Liu](https://xueyuhanlang.github.io/), Dr. [Yue Dong](https://yuedong.shading.me/) and Dr. [Xin Tong](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=P91a-UQAAAAJ) on 3D-related research problems.

My primary research interests lie in computer graphics and 3D vision, with a focus on 3D generation, rendering, and modeling. I am particularly interested in building scalable and controllable methods for neural 3D content creation.
